## Gap #1: AI Receptionist / Voice AI for Local Service Businesses

**Keyword/topic:** "AI receptionist for small business," "AI phone answering service," "AI voice agent for [vertical]"

**Why it's a gap:** We have a missed-call text-back post but nothing on AI voice agents that actually answer calls, qualify leads, and book appointments in real time. This is the hottest category in SMB AI automation right now — competitors like Smith.ai, Bland AI, and dozens of GoHighLevel agencies are publishing heavily here.

**Estimated search volume tier:** High (10K+ monthly searches across variations)

**Buyer intent:** High — searchers are actively looking for a solution to implement

**Recommended post angle:** "What an AI Receptionist Actually Does for a Local Service Business (and What It Doesn't)" — practical breakdown of capabilities, limitations, cost comparison vs. hiring a receptionist ($3,500/month) vs. answering service ($500–$1,500/month) vs. AI voice agent ($200–$500/month). Target HVAC, plumbing, and law firms specifically.

**Keyword/topic:** "automation ROI calculator," "cost of manual processes small business," "is automation worth it for small business"

Competitors use ROI calculators and cost-comparison content as top-of-funnel lead magnets. Our bottleneck audit post is close but doesn't put dollar figures on the cost of inaction.

**Estimated search volume tier:** Medium (1K–3K monthly searches)
