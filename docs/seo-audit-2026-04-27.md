## Issues NOT Fixed — Manual Attention Required

### P2 Items

**1. `speed-to-lead` H1 keyword gap**
- H1: "Why the First Business to Respond Wins 78% of the Time"
- seoTitle (title tag): "Speed to Lead: Why Fastest Response Wins" ✅
- The H1 body title is compelling and has a high CTR — rewriting it would risk engagement loss. The concept "speed wins" appears at word ~80 in the body. **Recommendation:** leave H1 as-is; this is an acceptable GEO tradeoff. The seoTitle carries the keyword in the title tag.

**2. `dormant-customer-reactivation-automation` — keyword in first 100 words**
- The seoTitle keyword "automate customer win-back" doesn't appear in the first 100 body words (appears ~word 183). The body opens with churn statistics.
- **Recommendation:** Add a single sentence after the opening stat: "Automated win-back sequences are the fix — and this guide shows you how to build one that recovers 8–15% of lapsed contacts without new ad spend." This can be done in a follow-up sprint without disrupting the post's strong opening hook.

### P3 Items

**3. Blog index page title tag**
- Current: `title: 'Blog'` → renders as "Blog | SMB Automation"
- **Recommendation:** Change to `title: 'SMB Automation Blog: AI & Automation Insights'` (~51ch) for better keyword presence on the index page. File: `app/(site)/blog/page.tsx`.

**4. `app/sitemap.ts` — pages listed may not all be indexable**
- `/services`, `/services/strategy`, `/services/build`, `/services/scale`, `/work`, `/contact` are all included and verified to exist as TSX files. No action required, but confirm these pages have unique, non-duplicate content before Google's next crawl.

**5. Open Graph image tags**
- The `generateMetadata` in `app/(site)/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` does not include `og:image`. Google and AI assistants (Perplexity, ChatGPT Browse) use OG images for rich results.
- **Recommendation:** Add a dynamic OG image using Next.js `ImageResponse` or a static fallback per post. This is a significant GEO signal enhancement for the next sprint.

**6. `dateModified` missing from BlogPosting JSON-LD**
- The schema outputs `datePublished` but not `dateModified`. AI assistants use `dateModified` to determine content freshness.
- **Recommendation:** Add `dateModified: post.date` (or track actual modification dates) to the BlogPosting JSON-LD in `app/(site)/blog/[slug]/page.tsx`.
