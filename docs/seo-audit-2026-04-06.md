## Issues NOT Fixed — Manual Attention Required

### P2 — Target keyword absent from first 100 words

**`ai-receptionist-local-service-business`**  
The post opens with a hook about $126,000/year in missed call revenue. "AI receptionist" first appears around word 135. Fixing this requires rewriting the opening paragraph, which falls outside automated prose changes. Recommended: prepend a one-sentence lede containing the target keyword before the current hook, e.g., *"An AI receptionist for your local service business answers every call, 24/7 — and here's how much you're losing without one."*

**`speed-to-lead`**  
The post opens with a setup paragraph about 78% of customers choosing the first responder. "Speed to lead" or "speed-to-lead" doesn't appear until the FAQ section. Fixing this requires editing the opening. Recommended: add `"Speed to lead — the time between a prospect contacting your business and receiving a response — is the single most important competitive variable in local service markets."` as an opening sentence.

### P3 — Blog index page metadata

`app/blog/page.tsx` uses a static metadata description: *"Practical automation insights for local service businesses. No fluff, no jargon."* This is on-brand but not keyword-targeted. Consider updating to include terms like "workflow automation," "AI for small business," or a specific vertical like "HVAC, law firms, and healthcare automation."

### P3 — Open Graph images missing

No `og:image` is set in `app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` or `app/page.tsx`. AI assistants (especially Perplexity) use rich preview metadata. Adding a static or dynamic OG image per post would improve citation presentation in AI-generated results.

### P3 — `dateModified` missing from BlogPosting schema

The JSON-LD `BlogPosting` schema in `app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` includes `datePublished` but not `dateModified`. Google and schema.org recommend both. Adding `dateModified` (can default to `datePublished` if no update tracking exists) strengthens structured data for AI crawlers.
