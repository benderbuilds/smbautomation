## 8. Next Sprint Recommendations

1. **Add `FAQPage` JSON-LD** to `app/(site)/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` — highest GEO impact available. Parser already renders FAQ H2s; extract Q&A pairs and emit structured data alongside BlogPosting. One-file change, no MDX edits required.

2. **Add `dateModified` frontmatter field** to posts updated this sprint and update the BlogPosting JSON-LD generator to emit it. Improves freshness signals for both Google and AI citation engines.

3. **Audit healthcare cluster for cross-linking to local-services posts** — verify that at least 2 posts from the healthcare vertical (med-spa, dental, GLP-1, HRT, TRT, semaglutide) link to topically adjacent local-services posts (e.g., appointment-reminder, ai-receptionist).

4. **Evaluate `cost-of-manual-processes.mdx` as a pillar page** — its broad intent ("cost of not automating") could anchor the full automation topic cluster. Adding a structured "key areas" section with links to 8–10 vertical-specific posts would increase its link equity and topical authority.
