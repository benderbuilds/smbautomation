Where commercial cleaning revenue disappears

Commercial cleaning companies have some of the highest-value recurring contracts in service businesses. The conversion problem isn't usually price or quality — it's follow-up discipline. Most owners simply don't have time to chase 20 open quotes simultaneously.

  • Quote follow-up is manual. The owner sends a quote and maybe follows up once. The company that follows up 5 times professionally — at days 3, 7, 14, 21, and 30 — wins the contract.
  • Contract renewals slip quietly. Clients whose contracts are coming up for renewal often go back to market without telling you. A proactive renewal outreach 90 days out keeps the contract yours.
  • Referral relationships go cold. Property managers, facility managers, and real estate agents who've referred business in the past stop referring when they don't hear from you. A quarterly check-in fixes that at zero cost.
  • No system for large prospect nurture. A prospect managing 50,000 sq ft of office space won't make a quick decision. Most companies don't have a long-game nurture for high-value targets.
What we build for your company
Quote follow-up automation
A 5-touch follow-up sequence triggered the moment a quote is sent. Each message adds value — a reference list, a case study, an availability reminder. Professional persistence without the manual effort.
Contract renewal alert system

Referral source nurture
