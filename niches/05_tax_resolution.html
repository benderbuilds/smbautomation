Why tax resolution firms lose cases they should win

Tax resolution has an unusual dynamic: the prospect is in acute pain, has a short decision window, and is actively comparing options in real time. The firm with the fastest, most authoritative response wins the case. Most firms aren't set up for that.

  • Response time kills more deals than price. Research shows conversion drops by 80% after the first hour. If someone just opened an IRS notice and called you at 7pm, a callback the next morning is too late.
  • Qualification takes too long. Owners spend hours on calls with leads who aren't actually closeable. An automated intake that qualifies urgency and case type gets you to the right conversations faster.
  • Document collection stalls cases. After signing, the time between engagement and case submission bleeds revenue. An automated document request and follow-up sequence cuts that window in half.
  • No triage system. A $15K OIC and a $3K penalty abatement require different urgency levels. Most firms treat every lead the same way.
